Project

We offer research based modular co-creation processes taking cultural experience into account.
It may result in differente products & services and(or) develop into a progressive course program.

1. Contract:

From a budgeted briefing & respectful meetings we structure a transparent legal contract for our partnership.

2. Management:

With our contract we organize our plan & sync schedules for our collaboration in every administrative detail.

3. Field-Research:

Through your enterprise's work history we research our best possible ecumenic cultural actions together.

4. Analysis:

Through social interfaces and data-science we shall study the relations among the concearning stakeholders.

5. Creative Program:

Solidary open-source practical workshops to collaboratively design & architect an ecumenic systemic artwork project.

6. Production Engineering:

Processualy documented sustainable scalable museologic operatic productions to foster the whole chain value ecosystem.
