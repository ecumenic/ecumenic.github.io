Foundation Reasons:

[Founded in 08.02.2019, Opera Day.]

The founder of this enterprise is a confessed sinner artist and holds no claim to be a model of conduct of any sort, but asks you to help him improving in being a better man. He is no master, guru, preacher, minister, healer, shaman, pai-de-santo, oracle, psychic, monk, nor any sort of spiritual arts guide or conductor although he reads tarot, iching, and astrological charts in a transpersonal archetypical creative way.

He heard many wise people in the spiritual path and studied as much as he could of the endless sea of holy teachings. But he is still an artist and creates poethic connections between spiritual knowledges. These creations showed the multilevel governance complexity implied in the stablishment of a respectful creation that could foster a peacebuilding culture.

Devoted to Jesus Christ, his personal Buddha, he tries with the Ecumenic Creative Operations to fundament the possibility of a responsible visionary faith in accordance to the Holy Bible, the UN 2030 Agenda, and freedom of speech at the same time. We are looking after partners who can understand compassionately our limits in scientific & historic precision as they intertwine with our regular and continued effort in self-discipline and respect towards every culture researching our own mistakes and improving our creative production.
